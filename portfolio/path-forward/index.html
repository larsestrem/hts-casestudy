From prototype to live, in about 60 days

The hard part is already done: 120 pages exist. What remains is verification by the people who know the operation, a focused correction pass, and a launch, then the growth engine takes over.

  1. 1. ReviewWalk every page with the people who actually know, ops, maintenance, chief pilot, and correct the liberties a prototype inevitably takes.

Found
